Game Instructions

    > Type 'start' to begin the game.

    > Type 1-3 word commands to interact with the environment and to move between rooms.

    > Valid commands consist of an action verb and noun. The following are examples of action verbs that may be used:

    > go [north, south, east, west], look, examine, pick up, take, check.

    > Type 'help' to get a hint.
